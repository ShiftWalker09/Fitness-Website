Exercises for Training Splits

Push/Pull/Legs Training

  • Push: Bench Press, Overhead Press, Triceps Dips
  • Pull: Pull-Ups, Barbell Rows, Bicep Curls
  • Legs: Squats, Lunges, Deadlifts

Bro Split

  • Day 1: Chest – Bench Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Cable Flys
  • Day 2: Back – Deadlifts, Pull-Ups, Seated Rows
  • Day 3: Shoulders – Overhead Press, Lateral Raises, Rear Delt Flys
  • Day 4: Arms – Bicep Curls, Triceps Extensions, Hammer Curls
  • Day 5: Legs – Squats, Leg Press, Calf Raises
